About Me

Hello! My name is Jermaine Lamarr Cole aka "J. Cole". I was born and raised on a military base in Frankfurt, Germany and raised in Fayetteville, North Carolina. I have always had a passion for music at the age of 12. Inspired by rappers such as Nas, Canibus, and Eminem, Cole began filling notebooks with rhymes and learned to make beats on an 808 drum machine his mother bought for him.

Hobbies

Cole loves eating the lobster mac and cheese at Catch in New York City. He says Tupac is the greatest New Yorker of all time (Tupac was born in New York.)

Career

J. Cole has released five studio albums: “Cole World: The Sideline Story” (2011), “Born Sinner” (2013), “2014 Forest Hills Drive” (2014), “4 Your Eyez Only” (2016), and “KOD” (2018). He has won two Grammy Awards from seventeen nominations, a Billboard Music Award for Top Rap Album, three Soul Train Music Awards, and eight BET Hip Hop Awards. Each of his albums has received platinum certifications by the RIAA.Throughout his career, J. Cole has continued to evolve as an artist, experimenting with different styles and themes. He has also ventured into production, handling the majority of the production in his own projects and producing singles for artists such as Kendrick Lamar and Janet Jackson. In addition, he founded his own record label, Dreamville Records, and non-profit organization, the Dreamville Foundation.
